Pricing FAQs

Everything you need to know about our pricing, payment, and policies.

Never. The price you see is the price you pay. The only variation is breed-size-based pricing — larger dogs simply require more time, products, and often two groomers. All size-based pricing is disclosed upfront during booking confirmation. We will always tell you the final price before starting and never add charges without your approval.
We collect payment after your pet's session, once you're happy with the result. We accept cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), all major debit/credit cards, and net banking. For membership plans, the first month is billed on sign-up and subsequent months auto-debit on the same date each month.
Yes! We offer a 10% multi-pet discount when two or more pets from the same household are booked in the same session. Senior pets (8 years+) receive a 10% wellness discount on all services as our sessions are paced more gently for them. These discounts can be combined with bundle pricing but not membership discounts.
Rescheduling is free with at least 24 hours' notice. Late cancellations (within 12 hours) incur a ₹199 convenience fee. No-shows are charged 50% of the booked service price. Members receive one free no-show pass per year. We completely understand that plans change — just let us know as early as possible and we'll always find you another slot.
You can pause your membership for up to two months per year (e.g., for holidays or illness) at no charge. To cancel, simply give us 30 days' written notice after the 3-month minimum term. There are no cancellation fees — we want members who love being here, not ones who feel locked in.
PawPoints are earned on every rupee spent at PawSpa (products, services, and add-ons). 100 PawPoints = ₹10 in redemption value. Points are valid for 18 months from the date of earning. Points earned through the referral programme (250 points per referred friend) expire after 12 months. Your points balance is always visible in your member dashboard.
