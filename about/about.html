Our Origin

Why PawSpa Exists

In 2017, our founder Anjali Mehta — a veterinary nurse and lifelong dog lover — walked into a local grooming salon with her Cocker Spaniel, Biscuit. What she witnessed troubled her deeply: rushed handling, harsh chemicals, and pets visibly distressed. She walked out and never went back.

Instead, she converted her apartment's spare room into a tiny grooming space, armed with organic shampoos, a gentle dryer, and a whole lot of patience. Word spread fast. Within six months, the waiting list was three weeks long.

Today, PawSpa is a 12-groomer team operating across 3 cities, having transformed the grooming experience for over 5,200 pets. But the promise remains exactly the same as that first day — gentle hands, clean products, zero shortcuts.

Every pet that enters PawSpa leaves not just cleaner — but calmer, happier, and more loved.

— Anjali Mehta, Founder & Head Groomer
What We Stand For

Our Mission & Core Values

Every decision at PawSpa — from the products we stock to the way we hold a nervous cat — is guided by a single principle: pet welfare first, always.

🐾

Our Mission

To provide every pet with a safe, stress-free, and premium grooming experience — while equipping pet parents with the knowledge and tools to continue that care at home. We exist to raise the bar for what grooming should feel like.

Safety Above All

We use only certified, hypo-allergenic, and cruelty-free products. Our groomers are trained in animal behaviour and stress-free handling techniques.

Genuine Compassion

We treat every pet as if it were our own. Patience is non-negotiable. No pet is ever rushed, restrained harshly, or left alone in an unfamiliar space.

Radical Transparency

You'll always know exactly what was done, what products were used, and how your pet behaved. Every session ends with a full digital grooming report.

Continuous Excellence

All groomers attend quarterly training. We invest in the latest tools, study breed-specific techniques, and actively seek feedback after every session.

Community First

We partner with local shelters, support adoption drives, and offer discounted sessions for rescue animals. Giving back is part of who we are.

Eco-Conscious Care

Biodegradable packaging, water-efficient bathing stations, and a commitment to zero single-use plastics across all our locations.

Our Journey

From One Room to Three Cities

Seven years of growth, love, and a lot of fur.

🏠
2017

The Spare Room Begins

Anjali Mehta grooms her first paying client — a neighbour's Golden Retriever named Charlie — in a converted spare room in Bengaluru. Word spreads to the entire apartment complex within weeks.

🏪
2018

First Proper Salon Opens

PawSpa opens its first dedicated salon in Indiranagar, Bengaluru. Two grooming stations, three product shelves, and a waiting list stretching 4 weeks long from Day 1.

🌿
2020

Going Organic & Online

Pandemic restrictions drive PawSpa to launch at-home grooming services and switch entirely to certified organic product lines. The digital booking system launches, handling 200+ bookings in its first week.

🚀
2022

Expansion to Hyderabad & Pune

PawSpa opens two new locations, hires 8 additional certified groomers, and launches the Membership programme — 500 members sign up in the first month alone.

🏆
2024

Best Pet Salon — City Awards

PawSpa wins Best Pet Grooming Salon at the Bengaluru City Business Awards. We now serve 5,200+ pets, carry 3,200 active members, and maintain a 4.9★ average rating across all platforms.

Standards & Safety

Certified, Hygienic & Accountable

Every PawSpa location undergoes bi-annual hygiene audits. Our groomers hold internationally recognised certifications and we publish our audit reports publicly. Transparency isn't a marketing line — it's our operating standard.

IGGA & NCGI Certified Groomers
International & National Grooming Guild accreditation for all senior staff.
Sterilised Tools Between Every Pet
All scissors, clippers, and brushes are autoclaved or UV-sanitised between each appointment.
Organic & Hypo-Allergenic Products Only
Zero parabens, sulphates, or artificial fragrances. All products are lab-tested and pet-safe.
Vaccination Records Required
All pets must be up-to-date on core vaccinations before any appointment — protecting every animal in our care.
Climate-Controlled Grooming Suites
Temperature and humidity are regulated in every grooming area to keep pets comfortable year-round.
