You can book directly through our website using the "Book Appointment" tab on this page, via our app, by calling us at +91 (987) 654-3210, or by sending us a WhatsApp message. We'll confirm your slot within a few minutes.
For your pet's first visit, please bring their vaccination records (especially rabies, distemper, and parvovirus for dogs), any specific grooming instructions from your vet, and your pet's favourite toy or blanket to help them feel comfortable.
Session duration depends on the service and breed. A basic nail clip takes 20 minutes, a bath & blow dry typically takes 60 minutes, and a full grooming with spa treatment can take up to 2 hours. We'll give you an estimated time when you book.
Absolutely! We specialise in stress-free grooming. Our groomers are trained in calming techniques, we use aromatherapy, and we always move at your pet's pace. Please mention your pet's anxiety level when booking so we can plan accordingly.
We ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the appointment may incur a ₹100 booking fee. No-shows without notice will be charged 50% of the service cost.
Yes. All our products are vet-approved, pH-balanced for pets, and free from parabens, sulphates, and harsh chemicals. We stock breed-specific and medicated formulas. If your pet has known allergies, please inform us when booking.
