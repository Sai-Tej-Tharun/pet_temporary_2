The Full Grooming Package is PawSpa's most comprehensive grooming service — a complete, spa-like experience designed to keep your pet looking, smelling, and feeling their absolute best. Ideal for both first-time visitors and loyal regulars, this package covers everything your pet needs in a single, relaxed session.

Our certified groomers begin with a thorough pre-bath brush-out to remove loose fur and tangles, followed by a warm organic shampoo bath and deep conditioning treatment. After a gentle, professional blow dry, your pet receives a breed-appropriate haircut tailored to their coat type, followed by nail trimming and filing, ear cleaning with vet-approved solution, and a finishing spritz of pet-safe cologne. Every step is performed in our calm, clean, stress-free environment.

How the Session Works

01

Check-In & Consultation

Your pet is warmly welcomed by our team. We review their coat type, any skin sensitivities, and note styling preferences before starting.

02

Pre-Bath Brush & De-Tangle

We carefully work through the coat to remove knots, mats, and loose fur before getting them wet — making the bath far more effective.

03

Warm Organic Bath & Conditioning

A soothing warm bath using our premium paraben-free, cruelty-free shampoo, followed by a deep conditioning mask to nourish the coat.

04

Blow Dry & Brushing

Gentle, velocity-controlled blow drying that keeps stress minimal, followed by a thorough brush-out to achieve a perfectly fluffy finish.

05

Breed-Specific Haircut

Our certified groomers perform a tailored cut based on your pet's breed standard or your custom style preference.

06

Nails, Ears & Finishing Touches

Nail trim and filing, ear cleaning, a spritz of cologne, and your choice of a bandana or bow — your pet is now runway-ready!
