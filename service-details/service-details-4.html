Nail Clipping

Fast, painless, professional nail care for dogs & cats

20 minDuration
₹249Flat Rate
All SizesSuitability
3–4 weeksRecommended

Regular nail trims are one of the most important — and most overlooked — aspects of pet health care. Overgrown nails cause discomfort, affect posture, can curl into the paw pad, and make walking painful. Our professional nail clipping service keeps your pet comfortable and mobile.

We use high-quality stainless steel guillotine and scissor-style clippers appropriate to each pet's nail size and thickness. Our groomers are trained to identify the quick (the blood vessel inside the nail) to avoid any accidental nicks. After trimming, all nails are filed smooth with a diamond-tipped file, and we finish with a pet-safe paw balm applied to moisturise and protect the paw pads.

How the Session Works

01

Settle & Calm

We let your pet sniff the clippers and file, offer a treat, and take a moment to ensure they're relaxed before starting — particularly important for first-timers or anxious pets.

02

Quick Assessment

We examine all nails and dewclaws, check the quick length, and assess paw pad condition before deciding on the safest trim level for each individual nail.

03

Front Paw Trim

Starting with the front paws, we trim each nail carefully with professional clippers, staying safely clear of the quick using our trained eye and proper lighting.

04

Back Paw & Dewclaw Trim

Rear nails are trimmed and any dewclaws are carefully managed — dewclaws don't naturally wear down and must be monitored to prevent curling into the skin.

05

File, Smooth & Moisturise

All nails are filed to remove sharp edges, and a generous application of paw balm is massaged into the pads and between the toes for soft, healthy paws.

Occasional quick nicks can happen even with the most experienced groomers, especially with dark-coloured nails where the quick is invisible. We always have styptic powder on hand to stop any bleeding instantly and painlessly. It's a minor occurrence and heals quickly — we'll always inform you if it happens and apply appropriate first aid.
