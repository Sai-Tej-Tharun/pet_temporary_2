Our Hair Styling service is where art meets animal care. Each cut is planned in consultation with you — we consider your pet's breed standard, coat health, lifestyle, and your personal style preferences before we pick up a single pair of scissors.

Our master stylists are trained in both traditional breed-specific patterns and modern creative cuts. We use professional-grade Japanese steel scissors, thinning shears, and clipper systems to deliver a precise, comfortable, and beautiful result every single time. The session includes a pre-styling bath (shampoo and blow dry) to ensure the coat is perfectly prepped before the cut begins.

How the Session Works

01

Style Consultation

We discuss your vision — breed standard or custom style — and examine the coat's condition to advise on what's achievable and what will look best on your pet.

02

Pre-Style Bath

Every styling session begins with a shampoo and blow dry to ensure the coat is clean, fully dry, and perfectly brushed out — a clean coat cuts more evenly and looks sharper.

03

Body Cut

Our stylist works through the body systematically — clipper work first for length, followed by scissor work for shape and refinement, all while keeping your pet relaxed.

04

Head & Face Styling

The most delicate part — face framing, ear shaping, beard or eyebrow styling, and eye area trimming are handled with careful scissor work and a steady hand.

05

Detail & Finish

Paw pad tidy-up, sanitary trim, blending with thinning shears, final cologne spritz, and your choice of bandana or bow — your pet is now runway-ready.
