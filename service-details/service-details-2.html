Bath & Blow Dry

A refreshing, deep-clean experience for dogs & cats of all breeds

45 min Duration
₹499+ Starting Price
All Breeds Suitability
Bi-weekly Recommended

The Bath & Blow Dry is the essential foundation of every healthy pet hygiene routine. We begin with a thorough pre-rinse to loosen dirt and debris, followed by a warm lather using our range of organic, breed-specific shampoos — formulated to cleanse without stripping natural oils.

A nourishing conditioner is applied and allowed to penetrate the coat before a warm rinse. Your pet is then wrapped in a warm towel and moved to our drying station where a velocity blow dryer (set to a comfortable level) fluffs and dries the coat completely. A final brush-out removes any remaining loose fur, leaving your pet looking and smelling incredible.

How the Session Works

01

Welcome & Pre-Check

We check your pet's coat type, note any skin sensitivities, and select the ideal shampoo formula for their needs.

02

Warm Pre-Rinse

A thorough pre-rinse with comfortably warm water to open the cuticle and loosen dirt, pollen, and allergens from the coat.

03

Organic Shampoo & Conditioning

We work premium organic shampoo into a rich lather from nose to tail, then apply a deep-conditioning mask and let it work for 3–5 minutes.

04

Rinse & Towel Dry

A thorough rinse to remove all product residue, followed by a warm towel wrap to absorb excess moisture and keep your pet comfortable.

05

Professional Blow Dry & Brush-Out

Velocity blow drying at a pet-safe temperature, combined with a slicker brush-out to achieve a perfectly fluffy, tangle-free finish.
